Great Wall of China


The Great Wall of China is an immense defensive structure built to protect China from invasions. Stretching thousands of miles, it winds through mountains and deserts, symbolizing China's historical strength and determination.

Key Information

  • Location: Northern China
  • Total Length: Over 21,000 km
  • Initial Construction: 7th century BCE
  • Most famous sections built during: Ming Dynasty
  • Purpose: Defense against invasions

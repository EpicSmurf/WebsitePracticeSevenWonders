Great of Pyramid of Giza

                    

The Great Pyramids of Giza were built as monumental tombs for the pharaohs.These iconic structures continue to amaze historians, archaeologists, and visitors alike. From their precise construction to the mysteries surrounding their purpose, the pyramids are a testament to the indomitable human spirit.

Key Information

  • Pharoahs: Khufu, Khafre, Menkaure
  • Khufu's Pyramind - Largest (146.6m)

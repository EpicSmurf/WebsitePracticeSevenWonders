The New Seven Wonders of the World

Introduction

The New Seven Wonders of the World is a list of remarkable modern architectural and cultural landmarks, selected through a global poll conducted in 2007. Unlike the ancient wonders, which were mostly lost to history, these structures still stand today as testaments to human ingenuity and craftsmanship. The wonders include the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Great Wall of China, Petra, the Colosseum, the Taj Mahal, Christ the Redeemer, and the Lighthouse of Alexandria. These iconic sites span different continents and continue to captivate millions with their historical significance and breathtaking beauty.

The New Seven Wonders of the World:

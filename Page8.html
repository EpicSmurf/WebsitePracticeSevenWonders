Christ the Redeemer


Christ the Redeemer is an iconic statue of Jesus Christ overlooking Rio de Janeiro. Standing atop Mount Corcovado, it symbolizes peace and is one of the most recognizable landmarks in Brazil and the world.

Key Information

  • Location: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
  • Height: 30 meters (statue), 8 meters (pedestal)
  • Completed: 1931
  • Material: Reinforced concrete and soapstone
  • Designer: Heitor da Silva Costa
